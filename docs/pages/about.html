About Me

Hey! I'm Tom Nyuma (most call me Nyumat)

I'm a student and hobbyist of web development.

I'm currently a student at Oregon State University Studying there the past two years to earn a degree in Computer Science.

This is what I look like when I'm not coding.



I love to make stuff. This love developed through Roblox in the 7th grade. I used Lua to bring the many ideas I envisioned in roblox; life, self-studying anything I could to make my games better. Soon after, I began high-school, where I was lucky enoguh to take two AP Computer Science courses. Especially since my school was the only in the district to offer it. We learned javascript through code.org and java through eclipse.

To say I enjoyed the course(s) would be an understatement. These courses instilled in me a grit to learn, build, and deploy from a very young age. I began to see 'creation' as an outlet. Some days, I'd do poorly on a math test then the minute I get home start building something.. really, anything I could think of in the code.org studio.

Thankfully, with all the experience I had under my belt, it was only natural that I declared for a Computer Science Major come university applications.

I recieved a hefty scholarship to come here and I am thankful to have taken that commitment. I have great friends, get to intern at some of the best companies in the world, and can make my mother and late father proud.
